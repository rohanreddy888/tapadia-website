Welcome to Tapadia Diagnostics

Tapadia had humble beginnings with a single Centre at RTC Cross Roads, and was started with the primary aim of providing reliable diagnostics to patients at affordable price. Tapadia’s unique inculcation of quality, affordability, reliability and ethical practice has created new bench marks in the diagnostic industry towards a higher standard of diagnostic medicine.Slowly and steadily Tapadia has been growing and now has branches all over the city, which function on the same principle of ethical practice of medicine and putting the patients’ health before everything else.

0
0

Mission is to provide quality diagnostic services at an affordable cost using the latest technology in order to maintain the highest quality of work with a commitment to ethical practices. To become a leading diagnostic Centre is providing reliable diagnostics at affordable rates. To practice medicine ethically. To create an organization propelled by a desire to be the best and the most affordable. To create a platform to exchange ideas and knowledge for all those associated with us. To become a socially conscious diagnostic center which aims at improving the quality of life of all sections of society.

The laboratory is equipped with latest equipment incorporating the latest technologies to provide reliable results in a short time.

TECHNOLOGIES AVAILABLE

We perform Chemiluminescence Immuno Assay (CLIA), Biochemistry, Enzyme Immuno Assay (ELISA), Radioimmunoassay (RIA), Nephelometry, Flowcytometry and High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and many other tests.

At Tapadia we realize that it is our responsibility to give back to the society. Hence we take up a number of activities which we do for free or provide necessary diagnostic services to needy people at subsidized rates. It is our way of giving back to society and providing help to those who need it.

Some of our activities are
  • Conducting camps in school.
  • Conducting Thalassemia awareness and detection camps.
  • Conducting Anemia awareness and detection camps.
  • Conducting blood donation camps.
  • Providing affordable diagnostic services to dialysis patients, juvenile diabetic patients and patients suffering from thalassemia.
  • Concessional rates for senior citizens and needy patients.

At Tapadia we are constantly working towards achieving total customer satisfaction by reliable and affordable pathological testing services with state of the art equipment, accuracy, speed, integrity and reliability.

Following the above policy the following objectives have been achieved.
  • Providing diagnostic services of high quality at affordable rates.
  • Receiving feedback from the patients in order to ensure that we continuously improve ourselves in order to serve them better.
  • Training our employees and implementing quality system procedures.
  • Improving performance and efficiency by monitoring testing results and upgrading and training the skills of employees through CME programmes.
